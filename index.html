Frequently Asked Questions

1. I am not a Singaporean citizen, but am I eligible to participate in this Design Challenge?

Yes! As long as you have an idea for a FUN BIN and an interest to take part in this challenge, you can! There are 2 main categories, one for Secondary School students and the other is an open category where any individual or group (of up to four) is allowed to participate. Anyone can participate and contribute ideas of brilliance!

This competition is open to the residents of Singapore, where all enrolled members of the team must have a residential address in Singapore. They must also be present at all milestone events in the competition process. There must be at least ONE Singaporean citizen in all applications.

COLOUR SPLASH FUN BIN CHALLENGE

Hey you, yes you! Do you know any primary school student who might want to showcase their talent and creativity?
Or are you a primary school student yourself?
Now, here's your chance!

The COLOUR SPLASH FUN BIN CHALLENGE will be accepting entries from 23rd June 2014. Entering the competiton is simple. Simply download the template, fill in the participant's details and start designing in all colors and/or materials! Designing is not just limited to colour pencils (crayons, water colour, whatever you wish), but really, with anything up to the limits of your imagination! The only requirement? All that is printed on the template must be visible in the submission. Only students who are enrolled in a primary school at the point of submission are eligible to participate in the competition.
