Frequently Asked Questions

1. Who is eligible to participate in the Colour Splash Fun Bin Challenge?

The Challenge is open to all students who are enrolled in a Primary School at the point of entry submission. Immediate family members of the volunteers and associates of Avelife are not eligible to participate in the Challenge.

2. Can I more than 1 Colour Splash Fun Bin template?

Yes you can! A maximum of three entries per student is allowed, but any additional entries via multiple accounts will be considered void.

3. What materials can be used during the design of the Colour Splash?

Any design is allowed! You are not restricted to just colour pencils, crayons, or watercolours. You can even design the Colour Splash in 3D, let your creativity flow!

4. How do I enter into the competition?

Download and print out the Colour Splash Fun Bin Challenge template from our website under the 'How to Enter' tab. Fill in your name, school and class, contact number and email into the template for us to keep in touch with you. Design and decorate your bin by 31st July 2014 and submit a clear photo to funbin.entries@avelife.org. Everything that is on the Colour Splash template must be visible during the submission. You only need to submit a photo of your Colour Splash design.

5. Are there any design criteria involved with the submission of the template?

Everything that appears on the template is up to your own imagination, creativity and liking but do bear in mind the judging criteria if you want to stand a chance to win! Judging criteria can be found on our website under 'Challenge' and they include elements of Innovation, Attractiveness and Relevance to Singapore.

6. Is there any application fee involved with the submission of the Colour Splash Fun Bin template?

No fees or money is involved to participate in this challenge, so do invite all your friends and share your ideas and creativity on Singapore's bin designs!

7. How will I be informed if I win the competition?

All winners will be notified via phone or email to attend an award presentation ceremony on the 10th of August 2014.

8. If the original design template has been damaged, will I still be able to collect a prize?

The judging will be based solely on the photo/scan ted to Avelife. As long as the photo is deserving of a prize, you will still be notified of your win. However, we want to encourage you to keep your original art work safe, at least for the duration of the Challenge, as we want to have the entries displayed at a public space for everyone to see!

9. Am I able to win a prize for each of my 3 entries?

Unfortunately, every single participant is only able to win not more than one prize for themselves, but the multiple entries do increase each person's chance of winning! So do submit more and stand a chance to win these attractive prizes! Only the final three entries as received will be taken into consideration for judging.

10. What is the budding Fun Bin Designers prize listed on this website about?

It is a prize that rewards effort such as the amount of hard work and thought given into the designing of the respective Fun Bin entries! So do show your enthusiasm and you'll be rewarded with a little gift from us!

11. What will happen to my entry after the challenge has ended?

Outstanding entries will be displayed at a public space in Queenstown for everyone to admire. On this end, we hope that all who participate in this competition will keep their entries in mint condition for the purpose that they may be displayed for every one else to admire.
